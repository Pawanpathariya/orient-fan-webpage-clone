ONE-STOP BRAND FOR ELECTRICAL LIFESTYLE SOLUTIONS

Orient Electric Limited (OEL) is part of the diversified USD 3 billion Indian conglomerate CK Birla Group. A distinguished name in the consumer electrical space for almost seven decades, Orient Electric has established itself as a trusted brand for consumer electrical products in India. It offers a diverse portfolio of fans, lighting, home appliances, switches, and switchgear. OEL stock trades under the code "541301" on BSE and under the code "ORIENTELEC" on NSE. OEL is a nimble organisation focused not merely on carving out larger market shares but delivering compelling consumer experiences and creating markets niches through differentiated products.
